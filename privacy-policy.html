LinkFindr Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 26, 2026

The short version: LinkFindr does not collect, store, or share any of your personal data. Everything stays on your device and in your personal iCloud account.

Overview

LinkFindr ("the App") is developed by Alex Templet. This privacy policy explains how the App handles your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

Data Collection

LinkFindr collects no data from its users. Specifically:

  • We do not collect personal information
  • We do not collect usage analytics or diagnostics
  • We do not use advertising identifiers
  • We do not use cookies or tracking technologies
  • We do not have user accounts or registration

Data Storage

All data you create in LinkFindr, including saved links, projects, highlights, and preferences, is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data is under your full control and is never transmitted to us or any third party.

iCloud Sync

If you have iCloud enabled on your device, LinkFindr uses Apple's CloudKit to sync your data across your personal devices. This data is stored in your private iCloud account, managed entirely by Apple. We have no access to your iCloud data. You can disable iCloud sync at any time in your device's Settings.

For more information on how Apple handles iCloud data, please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy.

On-Device AI Processing

LinkFindr uses Apple Intelligence (on supported devices) to provide features such as automatic categorization, tagging, and link summaries. All AI processing occurs entirely on your device. No data is sent to external AI services, cloud APIs, or remote servers for processing.

On devices that do not support Apple Intelligence, the App uses local heuristic algorithms to provide similar functionality, also entirely on-device.

Network Requests

LinkFindr makes the following network requests, none of which involve sending your personal data:

  • Link metadata: When you save a link, the App fetches the webpage's title, description, and icon directly from the website URL you provided. This is the same request your browser would make when visiting the page.
  • Offline articles: When you choose to save an article for offline reading, the App downloads the webpage content from the URL you saved.
  • App updates: The App may check the Apple App Store to determine if a newer version is available.

Clipboard Access

LinkFindr can detect URLs on your clipboard to offer a quick-save option. This detection happens locally on your device. The App only reads your clipboard when you explicitly interact with the paste prompt. No clipboard data is stored or transmitted.

Extensions

LinkFindr includes a Share Extension and an Action Extension that allow you to save links from other apps. These extensions store data in a shared on-device container accessible only by LinkFindr. No data is shared with other apps or external services.

Third-Party Services

LinkFindr does not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics frameworks, advertising networks, or external services. The App is built entirely with Apple's native frameworks.

Children's Privacy

LinkFindr does not collect data from anyone, including children under 13. The App is safe for use by all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will post the revised version on this page with an updated date. Since we do not collect any contact information, we encourage you to review this page periodically.
